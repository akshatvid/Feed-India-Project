Feed India is an NGO working to empower less privileged sections of society with access to adequate food especially in this COVID-19 pandemic situation.

Feed India is an organization with the goal of improving food problem among less-privileged sections of the society.

For now Feed India is only in a few major cities of Uttar Pradesh in India.

OUR MISSION

Feed India believes that every individual deserves to have access to proper and adequate amount of nutritious and healthy food facilities.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide access to healthy food to less-privileged individuals, in our neighbourhood.

OUR VALUES

Feed India values the longterm happiness of individuals, mentors and the Feed India Volunteers.

HOW WE WORK

Feed India contacts the restaurants and messes around the city. A huge quantity of food is leftover at these places, which the owners simply throw away. Instead of simply throwing away the food they donate the food to us so that we can serve the food to the less priviledged individuals.

Also the donations collected are used for the purpose of collecting food, transporting services and also given to the very needy people.

We believe that each and every section of the society should have access to proper and adequate nutritious food facilities even in this Pandemic situation.

When many of the organizations that distributed food in slum areas stopped serving the people due to the pandemic Feed India showed up.

We take every precautions in making and distributing food that is from recording body temperature of each individual to wearing masks and gloves while working.

We are a team of selfmotivated individuals who think for the society at large before their selfneeds.
